Welcome to Xtreme Sports! We are an organization that encourages awesome families to participate in top tier physical activity together. This strengthens your relationships, keeps everyone healthy and fit, and gets you out of the house - all while being able to meet other like-minded families!

We rely on our community in order to provide our services, so please don't hesitate to donate!

Our community recommends many different extreme sports to participate in, including: Kendo, Base Jumping, Sand Boarding, Canyoning, and Obstacle Course Racing! Explore out site to find out about even more activities.

You can also find different locations along with our main office in Downtown New York.
